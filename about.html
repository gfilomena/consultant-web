About Me

Alessandro Marangi

International Speaker | Career Coach | Talent Acquisition Expert

Who I Am

Alessandro Marangi is a seasoned career consultant and senior talent acquisition expert with over 10 years of experience at top multinational companies like McKinsey, Novartis, and Microsoft. As a respected international speaker at business schools and global events, Alessandro is known for his pragmatic approach, offering real-world insights into HR and recruitment.

With a focus on AI, Diversity & Inclusion, and the evolving recruitment landscape, Alessandro provides valuable knowledge for both HR professionals and job seekers.

He offers invaluable support both to those navigating growth within their current company and those seeking new roles or expanding internationally or across international borders.

My Approach

With over a decade of experience in Human Resources, Talent Acquisition, and Executive Search, Alessandro's consulting approach is global, strategically driven, and results-oriented. He excels at providing impactful guidance, empowering middle managers and executives to excel, optimize team performance, and harness the unique potential of multicultural teams.

Speaking & Thought Leadership

As a Board Member at the Rome Business School, Alessandro is frequently invited to deliver keynote speeches and participate on panels. His engaging sessions, from intimate workshops to large-scale conferences, provide practical insights to help individuals and teams excel in today's recruitment world.
